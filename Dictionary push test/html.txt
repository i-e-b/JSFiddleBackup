<p>
This is a demonstration of using a simple heuristic to compact entropy in a dataset.
We are aiming to have the highest probability in packed into the smallest number of code words.
</p>
<p>
This particular method is very simple and fast, but can't represent pairings (such as 'th', 'e ') or any more complex patterns like that.
</p>
