<p>
An implementation of the <i>lehmer64</i> pseudo-random number generator
in JavaScript using BigInt.
This is based on https://github.com/lemire/testingRNG/blob/master/source/lehmer64.h
</p><p>
This PRNG gives very good statistical results with very simple code.
</p><p>
D. H. Lehmer, Mathematical methods in large-scale computing units.
Proceedings of a Second Symposium on Large Scale Digital Calculating
Machinery;
Annals of the Computation Laboratory, Harvard Univ. 26 (1951), pp. 141-146.
</p><p>
P L'Ecuyer,  Tables of linear congruential generators of different sizes and
good lattice structure. Mathematics of Computation of the American
Mathematical Society 68.225 (1999): 249-260.
</p>
